How do I use this faucet rotator?

To use our faucet rotator, simply click the "Let's Start Earning!" button on the top of this page. It will bring you to your first faucet. There, you either create an account, or input your Bitcoin address.

Then, simply answer the captcha correctly and click "Claim". The Bitcoin will be sent:

To your Faucetbox account, where if you reach 13,000 Satoshi an hour, it will automatically withdraw to your Bitcoin wallet.

Directly, straight into your Bitcoin wallet. Usually has a fee, or is sent weekly.

The payouts of each faucet go into your coinwall account, which will be sent to your bitcoin address every Friday.
